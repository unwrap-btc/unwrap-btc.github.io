Why unwrap here

Built for BTC-first exits

Unwrap BTC when you want to move out of a wrapped token position, compare the route before signing and keep control of the wallet throughout the swap.

Self-custody stays in your hands

You connect a wallet, review the unwrap route and approve from your side. The app is designed around the Bitcoin network as the destination asset, not an exchange account.

Clear path from wrapped exposure

Wrapped assets can live on smart-contract networks such as Ethereum. Unwrap BTC helps you compare the source asset, destination BTC and route details before the transaction moves forward.

Live costs before confirmation

The preview stays number-free until the app quotes your exact route. In the app, you can check miner fees, route timing and slippage settings with context from Bitcoin mempool data.

How it works

Unwrap BTC in three steps

The flow is direct: choose the asset you are unwinding, choose BTC as the receive side, then sign only after the app shows the live quote.

1

Connect your wallet

Open the app and connect the wallet holding the asset you want to unwrap. Wallet connection standards such as WalletConnect keep the session wallet-native.

2

Select BTC as the output

Pick the source token and BTC destination. The app prepares the route, highlights the network path and waits for your review.

3

Review and sign

Check the live quote, estimated completion window and network cost in the app, then approve from your wallet if the route fits your plan.

FAQ

Unwrap BTC questions

01

What does Unwrap BTC mean?

Unwrap BTC means moving out of wrapped BTC exposure and into BTC through a swap route. It is useful when you want BTC settlement while keeping the transaction flow wallet-controlled.

02

Is Unwrap BTC non-custodial?

Yes. You connect your wallet, inspect the route and sign transactions yourself. Wallet apps such as MetaMask let you confirm permissions before approval.

03

Which chains and assets are supported?

Supported assets and networks depend on live routing availability. Check the current list on the supported chains page before planning a BTC unwrap.

04

How long does an unwrap take?

Completion time depends on the source network, route liquidity and BTC-side confirmation conditions. The app shows the current estimate before you sign.

05

What does it cost to unwrap BTC?

The cost comes from the live quote, route spread and network fees at confirmation time. The page does not publish fixed fees because the final route is calculated in the app.

06

How should I check safety before signing?

Confirm the asset, destination, route and wallet permissions before approval. If you are unsure, review each transaction screen carefully and avoid signing unfamiliar permissions.
